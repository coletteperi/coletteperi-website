Meet Colette

Colette Peri is a director and stop-motion artist known for turning everyday objects into joyful visual stories. Her work blends vibrant color, handcrafted details, and playful movement, bringing products to life in campaigns for Pottery Barn Kids, Sony, Starbucks, 3M and more.

Stop-motion is her signature craft, prized for the way it can make a product moment feel surprising and tactile, but she directs live talent and on-location shoots with the same instinct for creative storytelling, with a particular knack for working with kids on set. The result is work that performs across both social feeds and big screens: content people actually want to watch, built around a clear creative idea.

Beyond the camera, Colette speaks to audiences about creativity, easy stop-motion, and social-media boundaries for creatives. She's spoken at Adobe MAX, Alt Summit, and Sony Kando, along with private talks for companies like Capital One and Intuit.
